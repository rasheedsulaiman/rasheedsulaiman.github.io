in advertising

The Decision Marketplace isn't just another optimization engine, it's a fundamentally different approach to automated advertising intelligence that delivers stronger results for both advertisers and publishers.

Specialized Intelligence

+

Competitive Ecosystem

Agents are continuously evaluated based on the outcomes of their decisions, and competition ensures that good ideas are elevated and poor performers are retired.

+

Signal Agnostic

+

Dynamic Infrastructure

Modular architecture enables speed, access, and adaptability. Existing recipes combine, break apart, and reform into new configurations, so agents aren't starting from scratch with each new problem.
